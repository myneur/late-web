Privacy Policy

Information we collect, store and use and our Service Providers

  • On our servers: we don't store any personal information on our servers, we store only an anonymized subscription and device identifier. Service provider: heroku.com.
  • Inside your watch, we store: your calendar identifiers (typically an e-mail) and related calendar events, anonymous subscription and device identifiers and your location. Service provider: garmin.com might access the data during a setup or data loading.
  • We use stripe.com for subscriptions and payments. Stripe securely stores your credit card or mobile payment information and your e-mail and your subscription identifier. We don't have an access to your payment information.
  • We collect feedback, reviews and e-mails that you provided to us on Google Gmail and productboard.com.

By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of this information in accordance with this policy.

If you want to purge your data:

  • To clean data inside your watch, uninstall the watch face
  • Unsubscribe here or write us to help@sl8.ch
  • To erase a feedback that you provided to us, write us to help@sl8.ch

Transfer of data

Your information above, especially that stored on the mentioned service providers, may be maintained on computers located outside of your state or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ than those from your jurisdiction.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and mentioning the update on Garmin Connect IQ store of the watch face.
